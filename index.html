Welcome to Upbuild!

"Every journey begins with a signle step." -Lao Tzu

We are all on our own personal journey. Upbuild will help you get to where you want to be.

How Upbuild Works

Upbuild is designed to keep your focus on your Ultimate Goal each day by asking you to create goals of various levels. Each goal is a stepping stone to help you reach your final destination.

1/6

How Upbuild Works

You'll start by entering your Ultimate Goal - the vision of who you want to become. You can be as broad or specific as you'd like, just keep it real to you.

2/6

How Upbuild Works

Next, you will create a few Annual Goals that point to your Ultimate Goal. These should identify specifics you want to accomplish this year.

3/6

How Upbuild Works

You will then create some Monthly Goals that will lead to your Annual Goal. After each month, you will evaluate how you are progressing to your Annual Goal and then create a new Monthly Goal.

4/6

How Upbuild Works

Finally, you will be asked to create Weekly Goals for each Monthly Goal. During this process, you will identify on which days during the week you will need to complete the Weekly Goal.

5/6

Let's Get Started!

Now that you know how Upbuild works, jump on in and begin tracking your own progress as you work to become your best self.
